Overflow

it is used to hide/scroll the overflow content

it will work for block level elements and we need to mention a particular height

its a property of class

        overflow: visible/hidden/scroll/auto;
        overflow-x:(left/right)
        overflow-y:(top/bottom)

this is visible/default
